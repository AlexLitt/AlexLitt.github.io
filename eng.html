Carbon black:

The classification according to ASTM D1765 is based on the ability of some grades of carbon black to change the vulcanization rate of rubber compounds. Depending on this, the brands were assigned the letter indices “N” (with a normal vulcanization rate) and “S” (with a slower vulcanization rate). Following the alphanumeric index is the number of the group of marks on the average specific surface.

Silicon concentrate TU 38.32-001-47665149-2017:
